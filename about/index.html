About Me

I was born in Rome in 1999. Since I was a kid
I always liked drawing, and growing up I started
writing graffiti. Graphic Design seemed like the
most correct choice of studies after the diploma,
so I got into it, trying to figure out what exactly
I wanted to do with it. And that's how I discovered
animation, and my passion for it; from logo animations
to character rigging, I try to produce good looking,
smooth and engaging animations. Have a look at my works
and feel free to contact me if you need a new intro for
your YouTube channel or a lyric video for your song.
